# Further Reading & Resources

## Textbooks

### Bayesian Statistics (General)
- **Gelman et al.** — *Bayesian Data Analysis* (3rd ed.) — The reference text
- **MacKay** — *Information Theory, Inference, and Learning Algorithms* — Free online
- **Sivia & Skilling** — *Data Analysis: A Bayesian Tutorial* — Excellent for physicists

### Astrophysical Data Analysis
- **Gregory** — *Bayesian Logical Data Analysis for the Physical Sciences* — Directly applicable
- **Ivezić et al.** — *Statistics, Data Mining, and Machine Learning in Astronomy* — Comprehensive
- **Wall & Jenkins** — *Practical Statistics for Astronomers* — Accessible introduction
